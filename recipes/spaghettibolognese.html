Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A classic Italian dish featuring a rich and hearty meat sauce served over perfectly cooked spaghetti.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onions and minced garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Optionally, add red wine for extra flavor.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
