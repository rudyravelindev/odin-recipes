Souvlaki

Description:

Souvlaki is a delicious Greek dish consisting of skewered and grilled meat. This recipe features tender marinated meat, typically served with pita bread and a refreshing tzatziki sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken or lamb, cubed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Add the cubed meat to the marinade, ensuring it's well-coated. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for better flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill and thread the marinated meat onto skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and has a nice char.
  5. Serve with pita bread and tzatziki sauce.
