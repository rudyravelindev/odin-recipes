Chicken Salad

Description:

Enjoy a refreshing and flavorful chicken salad featuring juicy mango chunks, crisp vegetables, and tender chicken breast, all tossed in a tangy honey-lime dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded or diced
  • 1 large mango, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup toasted cashews or almonds, chopped (optional)
  • Mixed salad greens or spinach leaves for serving

Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken breast, diced mango, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, and chopped cilantro. Toss gently to mix.
  2. If using, sprinkle the toasted cashews or almonds over the salad mixture.
  3. In a small jar or bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, honey, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the honey-lime dressing over the chicken and mango salad, tossing gently to coat all ingredients evenly.
  5. Arrange a bed of mixed salad greens or spinach leaves on serving plates.
  6. Spoon the mango chicken salad over the greens and serve immediately, garnished with additional fresh cilantro if desired.
