Grilled Za'atar Chicken

Description:

This Grilled Za'atar Chicken is a flavorful and aromatic dish that features the Middle Eastern spice blend, za'atar. The combination of herbs and spices gives the chicken a unique and delicious taste.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons za'atar spice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, za'atar spice, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Coat the chicken breasts with the marinade, ensuring they are well-covered. Allow to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill and cook the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  4. Serve with a side of couscous or a fresh salad.
