Trinidadian Curry Chickpeas with Roti

Description

This Trinidadian-inspired curry chickpeas dish is rich, flavorful, and vegan-friendly. Served with soft, flaky roti, it's a hearty meal that celebrates Caribbean and Indian influences.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas (or 1 can, drained and rinsed)
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 roti (store-bought or homemade)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for 30 seconds.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until fragrant and golden brown.
  3. Stir in curry powder and turmeric, then add diced tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes break down.
  4. Add chickpeas and coconut milk. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with warm roti on the side.
